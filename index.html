About Me

I was born and raised in Ethiopia, graduated in Agro-Mechanization Engineering from Debub University in 2005 with a B.S. Since then I was working in the fields of Robot Tender, Sealer Deck and Sanding Tech at Nissan North America.

I decided to attend Nashville Software School to jumpstart my programming education in a place where I could work with like-minded people and learn best practices. Over the last six months, I’ve been rapidly building my skills as a full-stack developer and getting more excited every day about learning new technologies and finding ways to improve my code.
